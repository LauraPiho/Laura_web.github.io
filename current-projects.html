Current Projects

AutoMap Extreme

The concentration of the project is data validation, analysis and mapping for applications in extreme environments.

Key Objectives:

  • Develop robust data validation algorithms for extreme conditions
  • Create advanced mapping solutions for harsh environments
  • Implement real-time data analysis systems
  • Ensure data integrity in challenging operational conditions
Status: Active | Duration: 2024-2026

The Iliad Project

The EU-funded ILIAD project is building an interoperable Digital Twin of the Ocean to simulate climate scenarios and support decision-making. By integrating diverse data sources and models, it enables accurate forecasting of marine systems.

Key Features:

  • Digital Twin of Ocean systems
  • Climate scenario simulation
  • Multi-source data integration
  • Decision support tools for marine management
  • Advanced forecasting capabilities
Funding: EU Horizon 2020 | Status: Active

Collaboration & Impact

These projects represent cutting-edge research in environmental monitoring and marine sciences, combining advanced signal processing techniques with real-world applications in extreme and underwater environments. The work contributes to better understanding and management of our changing planet.
