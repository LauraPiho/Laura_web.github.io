About

I am a researcher at the Center for Biorobotics, Tallinn University of Technology. The focus of my research is signal processing and data analysis for environmental applications. The main concentration of my work lies in extreme and underwater environments.

Current Projects

AutoMap Extreme

The concentration of the project is data validation, analysis an mapping for applications in extreme environments.

The Iliad Project

The EU-funded ILIAD project is building an interoperable Digital Twin of the Ocean to simulate climate scenarios and support decision-making. By integrating diverse data sources and models, it enables accurate forecasting of marine systems.
